Our Story

A Coastal Haven

Nestled in the heart of a charming fishing village, Coastal Lodge offers a perfect blend of luxury and authentic coastal experiences. Founded in 2005 by the Johnson family, our lodge has been designed to provide guests with ultimate comfort while preserving the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region.

Each aspect of our lodge reflects our commitment to excellence, from the carefully curated interiors to the locally-inspired cuisine served at our restaurant. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or a base to explore the stunning coastline, Coastal Lodge provides an unforgettable experience for all our guests.

Over the years, we have grown from a small guesthouse to a premium accommodation destination, while maintaining our dedication to personalized service and authentic experiences. Our team takes pride in creating memorable stays for each guest who walks through our doors.
